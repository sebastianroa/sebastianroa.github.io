About Us

Hi there! Thanks for stopping by and checking out the Max Pet Viewer team. We are a startup consisting of 9 individuals, all of whom balance the duties of marketing or computer programming. Based in Gainesville, Florida, we have team members on two continents. We believe this diversity introduces a synergy that enables our organization to introduce innovative ideas to the dog daycare industry. One of those goals is to become the premier dog daycare viewing experience. We want to make it an easy, accessible, and entertaining experience for your customers. We would also like to take the time to share some of our visions for the industry and how we plan to change the Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model for the better.

Software as a service is broken.

At Max Pet Viewer, we will admonish that philosophy! We believe the moment you invest a flat fee into our services, you should be entitled to all the upgrades and new features that come out.
